FAQ

Common questions,
answered honestly.

How do I access the handbooks after purchase? +
All handbooks, blueprints, and labs are hosted directly on our online platform. Once you purchase or subscribe, you'll receive credentials to log in and read all content in your browser with collapsible sections and copyable code configs.
Why online HTML instead of static PDFs? +
Online HTML allows us to embed interactive code components, collapsible sidebars, and tabbed code blocks (like switching between AWS and GCP). It also ensures you always read the latest, verified API version without having to download updated files.
Can we share these resources within our team? +
Individual purchases and Academy Passes are licensed to a single user. For team sharing, choose the Enterprise Pass which supports up to 10 active developers.
Is there a refund policy? +
Yes. If you're not satisfied with a handbook or lab, email us within 14 days for a full refund. Refunds are not available for users who have actively accessed more than three premium modules under an Academy Pass.
