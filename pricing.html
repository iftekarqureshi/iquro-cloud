FAQ

Common questions,
answered honestly.

What formats are the publications delivered in? +
All technical publications are delivered in DRM-free PDF and EPUB formats, and code templates are provided as clean zip archives containing copy-pasteable Terraform or GitLab/GitHub workflows.
Can we share these resources within our team? +
Individual purchases and Monthly Passes are licensed to a single developer. For sharing across teams, please choose our Enterprise Access tier, which supports up to 10 active developers.
Is there a refund policy? +
Yes. If you are not satisfied with a publication, email us within 14 days of purchase for a full refund. Please note refunds are not available for users who have downloaded more than three files under a Monthly Pass.
How often is the catalog updated? +
We publish new blueprints and runbooks monthly, addressing new cloud provider releases, security threat updates, and modern pipeline security practices.
