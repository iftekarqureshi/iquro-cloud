FAQ

Common questions,
answered honestly.

How does the trial access work? +
We provide trial access to our core templates (AppFrame and standard Terraform modules) within a sandbox environment. Your engineering team can test and deploy the templates to verify core capabilities before purchasing an enterprise license.
Can we customize the templates? +
Yes. All modules and templates are distributed as clean, version-controlled source code. You are free to fork, edit, and extend the configurations to match your organization's unique requirements.
What happens if we need custom integrations? +
Our Enterprise Custom tier includes dedicated platform engineer hours. We will build custom Terraform modules for your private services, integrate custom GitOps workflows, or help migrate legacy monoliths.
Do you sign NDAs? +
Always. Confidentiality of client infrastructure and business logic is standard practice — not an optional add-on. Our current engagement with a major GCC banking institution is conducted entirely under NDA.
What timezones do you serve? +
IQuro Cloud operates from Muscat, Oman (GST, UTC+4) and is available for US, EU, and APAC clients with flexible overlap hours. Most communication is async by default — we document everything — with synchronous sessions scheduled around your timezone.
