FAQ

Common questions,
answered honestly.

How do you handle project scoping? +
Every engagement starts with a free Technical Discovery — typically 60–90 minutes — where we map your requirements, existing infrastructure, and business constraints. We produce a detailed technical specification and a fixed-price proposal. You know the total cost before signing anything.
Do you work on existing codebases? +
Absolutely. Roughly half our engagements involve existing systems — performance issues, deployment problems, legacy modernization. We'll do an honest technical assessment of what exists before recommending any approach.
Can I start small and scale the engagement? +
Yes. Many clients start with a focused engagement — a cloud architecture review, a CI/CD pipeline build, or an MVP — and expand from there. Every engagement is designed to stand alone and integrate cleanly with whatever comes next.
Do you sign NDAs? +
Always. Confidentiality of client infrastructure and business logic is standard practice — not an optional add-on. Our current engagement with a major GCC banking institution is conducted entirely under NDA.
What timezones do you serve? +
IQuro Cloud operates from Muscat, Oman (GST, UTC+4) and is available for US, EU, and APAC clients with flexible overlap hours. Most communication is async by default — we document everything — with synchronous sessions scheduled around your timezone.
